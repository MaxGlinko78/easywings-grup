Household chores - tasks such as cleaning, washing, and ironing that have to be done regularly at home.
To do the dishes -Wash the plates, glasses, and silverware used at a meal.
To water the plants - to pour water on to plants or the soil that they are growing in.
To mow the lawn - to cut the grass on a lawn with a lawn mower. To scrub the toilet - to rub hard with a brush, cloth, etc., or against a rough surface in washing.
To tidy up the room - to make a place look tidy.

Тема 2.

Жизнь в городе и сельской местности. Проблемы города и села.
Some Useful Phrases:
Urban wasteland – a city area which is empty and in a bad condition.
No-go arrears – where the police and other authorities are afraid to enter.
Run-down buildings – in a very bad condition.
Deprived areas – not having the things that are necessary for a pleasant life.
Sprawling city – spread over a large area (negative) Bumper-to-bumper traffic – so many cars and so close that they are almost touching each other Incessant roar – very loud noise which never stops, Comfortable suburbs – a place from which many people travel in order to work in a bigger city.
Shanty towns – very poor houses made of discarded materials.

Тема 3.

Общение в семье и школе, семейные традиции, межличностные отношения с друзьями и знакомыми.
Some Useful Phrases:
Reluctant – not wanting to do sth.
Bond – a connection between people based on shared feelings or experiences.
Respect (for sb) – a feeling of admiration for sb because of their qualities.
Confide in sb – tell sb personal information because you trust them.
Ups and downs – a mixture of good and bad things in life or a relationship.
Appreciate sth – recognize sth and welcome it.
Make a sacrifice – give up sth important or valuable in order to do sth that seems more important.

Some Useful Phrases:
Medical care - help given to someone who's sick or injured.
Be discharged from hospital - to be allowed to leave the hospital.
A splitting headache - a very bad headache.
Premature aging- abnormally early appearance of signs associated with old age.
Preventive medicine – the branch of medicine concerned with averting disease.
Communicable disease – any condition that is transmitted (as from person to person) by direct contact with an affected individual.
Come back rejuvenated – to return feeling yourself young and energetic again.
Conventional medicine - the usual methods of healing and treating disease.
